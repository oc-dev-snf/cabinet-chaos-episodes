# THE THICK OF ITCH

**Episode:** 017  
**Title:** The Whisper Vote  
**Genre:** Dark political satire

---

## COLD OPEN – INT. HOUSE OF COMMONS CHAMBER – 14:03

The lights dim without warning.

**PM HALVORSEN** stands at the dispatch box, smile polished, voice soft.

**HALVORSEN:**
Order. Today, we vote on stability.

No one asks what that means. They have already seen the amendment.

---

## Premise
A "stability" bill arrives with hidden coercion baked into the vote. MPs receive timed threats tied to private archives, party discipline mutates into strategic blackmail, and Parliament passes a law nobody dares defend out loud. By evening, the blackmail files never drop—because the operation was only a test.

---

## ACT 1 – THE FOLDER

Minister Graves slides a black folder to front bench leaders.

Embossed in silver:

> **COMPLIANCE**

Opposition Leader Voss opens hers.

- Page 1: blank
- Page 2: **"If this bill fails, your private archives are released at 18:00."**

She looks up. Graves is already watching.

---

## ACT 2 – THE BUZZING CHAMBER

Phones vibrate in sequence across both sides of the house.

Each message carries a different private exposure:

- tax irregularities
- private recordings
- affairs
- deleted chats restored
- procurement signatures

Every message ends the same way:

> **VOTE YES. KEEP ORDER.**

The Speaker calls for calm. Nobody is listening.

---

## ACT 3 – PROCEDURE AS WEAPON

In a corridor, junior whip Lena Pike confronts Graves.

**LENA:**
You’re blackmailing Parliament.

**GRAVES:**
No. I’m simplifying decision-making.

Inside the chamber, Voss rises.

**VOSS:**
This is not a stability bill. It is a leash.

Half the room flinches.

Halvorsen replies with legal theatre.

**HALVORSEN:**
Are you alleging misconduct?

**VOSS:**
I’m alleging cowardice.

---

## ACT 4 – THE BOARD

The vote board lights:

Green. Green. Green.

A pause. Two red votes appear.

Then more green, all at once.

> **BILL PASSED**

Graves taps his watch exactly as the final votes land.

---

## TAG – 18:07

No archive dump occurs.

All members receive one final message:

> **"Thank you for your cooperation. This was only a rehearsal."**

Lena stands at the window facing the government quarter. Office lights burn behind mirrored glass.

Somewhere inside, someone has learned democracy can be trained to kneel.

Somewhere else, someone is drafting the next vote.
