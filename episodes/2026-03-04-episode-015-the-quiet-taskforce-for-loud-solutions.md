# THE THICK OF ITCH

**Episode:** 015  
**Title:** The Quiet Taskforce for Loud Solutions  
**Genre:** Original political satire

---

## Premise
After a minister announces a new "Quiet Governance Taskforce" meant to reduce noise, conflict, and confusion in public life, Whitehall immediately descends into choreographed shouting, stealth briefings, and a procurement panic over government-issued whispering megaphones. As departments race to prove who can be calmest the loudest, policy is drafted in soundproof booths, leaked through a mindfulness app, and ultimately decided by whichever civil servant can ring a desk bell with the least constitutional fallout.

## COLD OPEN – INT. CABINET OFFICE CORRIDOR – 7:03 AM

A fresh sign is taped across a meeting room door:

> **QUIET GOVERNANCE TASKFORCE — SPEAK SOFTLY, GOVERN HARDER**

Inside, six advisers argue in aggressive whispers.

**MINISTER HOLT:**
No raised voices. We are a model of serene administration.

**DAN:**
You’re whispering like a man trying to negotiate with a smoke alarm.

**JULIA:**
Why are there fifty boxes labelled *TACTICAL HUSH EQUIPMENT* in reception?

A junior aide wheels in a chrome device shaped like a cone.

**AIDE:**
Prototype whispering megaphone, ma’am.

**JULIA:**
That is just a normal megaphone with velvet on it.

---

## ACT ONE – INT. TASKFORCE WAR ROOM – 8:16 AM

Walls covered in posters: **"CALM IS DELIVERY"** and **"NO COMMENT, JUST COMPETENCE."**

**NINA:**
We’ve had twelve requests for guidance on acceptable decibel levels for panic.

**DAN:**
Set panic to medium and publish a chart.

**HOLT:**
Good. Practical. Humane. Also, who approved this bell?

A brass desk bell sits at the head of the table beside ministerial folders.

**PRIVATE SECRETARY:**
Protocol says one ring means "consensus." Two rings means "urgent consensus." Three rings means "historical inquiry."

**DAN:**
And four?

**PRIVATE SECRETARY:**
Judicial review.

### SCENE 2 – INT. PRESS OFFICE – 8:41 AM

A comms board shows three draft lines for the same policy:

1. **"Government introduces calm leadership framework."**  
2. **"Government bans shouting near policy."**  
3. **"Government neither confirms nor denies audible governance."**

**JULIA:**
Why have we prepared contradictory statements again?

**PRESS OFFICER:**
Because each one tested well with a different focus group emotion.

**DAN:**
Which emotion chose option three?

**PRESS OFFICER:**
Exhaustion.

---

## ACT TWO – INT. PROCUREMENT HUB – 9:28 AM

Pallets of "soundproof pen dividers" and "certified neutral bells" fill the room.

**NINA:**
How much did we spend on hush logistics this morning?

**PROCUREMENT LEAD:**
Technically nothing. We reclassified it as preventative tranquility.

**DAN:**
That sounds expensive with better handwriting.

A monitor flashes: **TENDER CHALLENGE FILED — CATEGORY: BELL FAIRNESS**

**PROCUREMENT LEAD:**
Competing suppliers are alleging the chosen bell tone implies ideological bias.

**NINA:**
It’s a bell.

**PROCUREMENT LEAD:**
In governance, everything is a signal.

### SCENE 2 – INT. SOUNDPROOF POLICY BOOTH – 10:06 AM

Senior officials draft legislation through thick glass, communicating by marker pen.

One writes: **"MANDATE CALM BRIEFINGS"**

Another writes: **"DEFINE CALM"**

A third writes: **"CHECK IF LEGAL"**

They hold up pages at each other in silence.

**DAN (outside booth):**
We have successfully reinvented government as aquarium theatre.

**GOVERNMENT LAWYER (through intercom):**
Legal note: silence is not consent unless minuted.

---

## ACT THREE – INT. BROADCAST STUDIO – 11:19 AM

The minister rehearses a statement beneath a banner reading **"NATIONAL WEEK OF LOWERED TONES."**

**HOLT (measured):**
This administration is calm, coherent, and entirely in control—

A backstage producer accidentally hits the "ambient relaxation" track. Whale sounds fill the studio.

**JULIA:**
Cut the whales. Keep the sentence.

**DAN:**
No, leave them. It’s the most peaceful briefing we’ve had all year.

### SCENE 2 – INT. TASKFORCE WAR ROOM – 11:53 AM

A leaked screenshot appears online from a mindfulness app used by officials:

> **TODAY’S GOVERNANCE AFFIRMATION:**  
> *"You are not behind. You are merely pre-announced."*

**NINA:**
It’s viral.

**JULIA:**
In a good way?

**NINA:**
In a way that creates committees.

The desk bell rings once. Everyone freezes.

**HOLT:**
Who rang that?

No one answers.

The bell rings twice.

---

## CLIMAX – INT. CABINET OFFICE MAIN TABLE – 12:32 PM

Senior staff gather. Three policy versions are on the table, each stamped **FINAL**.

**CABINET SECRETARY:**
We cannot leave with three finals. One policy, now.

**DAN:**
Fine. We pick the least embarrassing option and call it strategic clarity.

**JULIA:**
Which one is least embarrassing?

**NINA:**
Depends whether Parliament prefers calm, certainty, or plausible deniability.

The desk bell is placed in front of the Cabinet Secretary.

**CABINET SECRETARY:**
One ring for option A. Two for B. Three for C.

He hesitates. Rings once. Then, immediately, twice by accident.

Everyone stares.

**DAN:**
Great. Coalition policy by percussion.

**CABINET SECRETARY:**
Minute it as a blended mandate.

---

## TAG – INT. NO.10 STAIRWELL – 6:08 PM

Dan and Julia sit on the stairs with takeaway coffee.

**JULIA:**
Update: the Quiet Governance Taskforce has been expanded.

**DAN:**
Into what?

**JULIA:**
The Department for Responsible Volume.

Nina appears, holding a small velvet case.

**NINA:**
Commemorative gift from procurement.

She opens it. Inside: a tiny brass bell engraved **"DELIVERY THROUGH DECIBELS."**

Dan sighs.

**DAN:**
Tomorrow we’ll launch a strategy for fewer strategies.

A distant bell rings somewhere in the building.

**END**
