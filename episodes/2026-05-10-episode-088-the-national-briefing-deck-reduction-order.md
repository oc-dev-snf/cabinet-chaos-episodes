# THE THICK OF ITCH

**Episode:** 088
**Title:** The National Briefing Deck Reduction Order
**Genre:** Original political satire

---

## COLD OPEN

(CABINET OFFICE INCIDENT ROOM, 07:00. A screen reads: **"TODAY'S BRIEFING PACK — 482 SLIDES"**.)

Morning briefing card:

**DELIVERY RECOVERY WEEK — DAY 4**  
**Scenario:** *"The National Briefing Deck Reduction Order"*

**PETER-LIKE MINISTER:** Why does this briefing have a table of contents for the appendices to the pre-read?

**PRIVATE SECRETARY:** Because every team added "essential context" and nobody wanted to remove anyone else's chart.

**OPERATIONS ADVISER:** We also have three slide versions titled "FINAL_FINAL_v9_USE_THIS_ONE".

**PETER-LIKE MINISTER:** Perfect. We are now governed by presentation archaeology.

CUT TO TITLE.

## ACT ONE — DECK INFLATION

08:22. Cross-department prep call with policy, delivery, finance, and comms.

Slide title: **"CLARITY PER MINUTE: CURRENTLY NEGATIVE"**

**PM:** How long does it take to read this pack?

**DELIVERY DIRECTOR:** About four hours, assuming no emotional response to pie charts.

**CHIEF OF STAFF:** Meeting length?

**DELIVERY DIRECTOR:** Forty-five minutes.

Three causes of deck inflation identified:

1. Every risk moved onto slides "for visibility"
2. Historical charts never retired
3. Decisions buried behind scene-setting and caveats

**PETER-LIKE MINISTER:** We haven't built briefing packs. We've built laminated weather systems.

## ACT TWO — THE REDUCTION ORDER

10:05. Rapid process session with private offices and analytics leads.

Slide title: **"DECISION FIRST, EVIDENCE ATTACHED"**

New pack rules approved:

- **Seven-Slide Core:** objective, decision, options, recommendation, risk, delivery impact, next action
- **Evidence Annex:** detailed charts moved to linked appendix, not live deck
- **Retirement Rule:** any chart unused for three cycles is removed
- **Single Owner:** one named editor per pack with authority to cut duplication

**LEGAL ADVISER:** Keep a full audit trail in annex notes.

**COMMS LEAD:** Keep the headline plain enough to survive daylight.

**PETER-LIKE MINISTER:** If the decision is on slide thirty-eight, the decision has already escaped.

## ACT THREE — FIRST LIVE RUN

12:14. New format trialled for the afternoon ministerial round.

**INCIDENT COORDINATOR:** Core deck reduced from 482 slides to 7. Annex has 126 reference slides.

12:47. Meeting begins.

**SERVICE OWNER:** We reached the decision point in eight minutes instead of thirty-two.

13:03. Annex used for one challenge on cost assumptions.

**FINANCE LEAD:** Better. Evidence is available without hijacking the agenda.

14:11. Follow-up checks:

- Conflicting numbers flagged and corrected in one place
- Duplicate charts dropped from four teams
- Action owners assigned before close of meeting
- Summary sent within 20 minutes, not end of day

**PM:** We may have discovered that shorter meetings are possible.

## ACT FOUR — COMMS WITHOUT THE WALL OF SLIDES

16:41. Communications line agreed: **"GOVERNMENT STREAMLINES BRIEFINGS TO SPEED DECISIONS"**

Public points:

- Concise packs improve accountability, not just speed
- Evidence remains accessible in full annexes
- Named ownership reduces version confusion
- Faster decisions still require documented rationale
- Common format helps departments challenge each other clearly

By 18:09 (day-one metrics):

- Average core briefing length down 91%
- Time-to-decision in pilot meetings down 63%
- Post-meeting clarification emails down 48%
- Version conflicts down 72%

**HEAD OF COMMS:** It sounds less heroic than an emergency reset, but it works.

**PETER-LIKE MINISTER:** The revolution is that people can now find page one.

## CLIMAX — THE LOBBY SCRUM

18:26. Reporters gather outside the briefing room.

**REPORTER:** Minister, are you dumbing down government papers?

**PETER-LIKE MINISTER:** No, we're separating the decision from the museum.

**REPORTER:** Is detail being hidden?

**PETER-LIKE MINISTER:** It's in the annex, indexed, and actually readable.

**REPORTER:** What's the practical effect?

**PETER-LIKE MINISTER:** Fewer performative slides, more accountable calls.
